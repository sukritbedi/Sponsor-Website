About Kamaraj Ecolastic Turf

Who are we?

Kamaraj Ecolastic Turf is a leading supplier and installer of Indoor and Outdoor Synthetic Ground and Grass related products serving to dealers, distributors, architects, interior and landscape designers, builders and developers and residential or corporate clients for the product requirements in homes, offices, weekend homes, townships, podium gardens, kids play areas, sports fields, etc. The company takes care of the supplies as well as the work execution on the site location.

Why Us?

We at KET, along with our suppliers, are constantly innovating to bring newer and better quality products to you and to the market. The USP is excellent quality and ready available stock at the most affordable prices. Products are certified FIFA, FIH, ITF, BWF Green building council and the products are also as per ISO standards.
